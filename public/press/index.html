Game Description

p(Doom)1 is a satirical meta-strategy game about AI Safety that challenges players to navigate the complex landscape of artificial intelligence development while managing existential risks. Players must balance research progress, policy decisions, and public perception while keeping the probability of doom (p(doom)) at manageable levels.

Key Features

  • Strategic gameplay focused on AI Safety scenarios
  • Economic simulation with resource management
  • Policy decision-making with real-world implications
  • Educational content about AI Safety concepts
  • Leaderboard system with community competition
  • Open source development model
