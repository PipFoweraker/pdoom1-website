About the Cat Custodians

In p(Doom)1, community contributors can earn special rewards including having a cat NPC named after them (or their cat!). These loyal felines serve as office companions, providing morale boosts and occasional insights into AI Safety research.

Want to become a custodian? Check out the contributors page to learn how to earn rewards.
