[h1] Privacy & Analytics
[p] Our commitment to privacy-preserving analytics
[h2] Privacy-First Analytics
[p] At p(Doom)1, we believe in respecting your privacy while understanding how our website is used. We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics service that helps us improve the website without compromising your privacy.
[h4] 🚫 No Cookies
[p] We don't use cookies or persistent identifiers.
[h4] 🔒 No Personal Data
[p] We never collect personally identifiable information.
[h4] ✅ GDPR Compliant
[p] Fully compliant with privacy regulations by design.
[h4] 🌍 Respects DNT
[p] We honor Do Not Track browser settings.
[h2] What We Collect
[p] We collect only aggregate, anonymous information to understand website usage:
[li] Page views: Which pages are visited (no user identification)
[li] Referrers: Where visitors come from (e.g., search engines, social media)
[li] Browser & OS: General browser and operating system information
[li] Device type: Desktop, mobile, or tablet (no device fingerprinting)
[h3] What We DON'T Collect
[li] ❌ IP addresses (anonymized immediately)
[li] ❌ Cookies or local storage (except for opt-out preference)
[li] ❌ Personal information (names, emails, etc.)
[li] ❌ Cross-site tracking data
[li] ❌ Fingerprinting data
[h2] Opt-Out Controls
[p] You can opt out of analytics at any time. Your preference is stored locally and will persist across visits. You can also opt back in if you change your mind.

[p] Note: If you have Do Not Track enabled in your browser, analytics are automatically disabled regardless of this setting.
[h2] Data Retention
[p] All analytics data is automatically deleted after 90 days. We don't retain long-term tracking data or build user profiles.
[h2] Analytics Provider
[p] We use Plausible Analytics, an open-source, privacy-focused analytics platform. Plausible is:
[li] Open source and auditable
[li] Can be self-hosted for full data ownership
[li] Lightweight and fast (<1KB script)
[li] Does not use cookies or track users across sites
[li] GDPR, CCPA, and PECR compliant
[h3] Alternative Providers
[p] We evaluated several privacy-preserving analytics solutions including:
[li] Plausible: Our chosen solution (lightweight, open-source)
[li] Umami: Self-hosted option with similar privacy features
[li] Simple Analytics: Privacy-focused with carbon-conscious hosting
[p] All three options are privacy-preserving and GDPR-compliant. We chose Plausible for its simplicity, open-source nature, and excellent documentation.
[h2] Questions?
[p] If you have any questions about our privacy practices or analytics implementation, please check our GitHub repository or contact us through the issue tracker.
