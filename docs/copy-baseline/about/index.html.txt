[h1] About p(Doom)1
[p] The story behind the AI Safety Strategy Game and our mission to make complex topics accessible through interactive gameplay
[h2] Our Mission
[p] p(Doom)1 was created to make AI Safety concepts accessible and engaging through interactive gameplay. We believe that games can be powerful educational tools, helping people understand complex topics by letting them experience the challenges firsthand.
[p] The game combines satirical humor with serious educational content, inspired by titles like Papers Please and Pandemic. Players navigate the complex world of AI development while managing resources, making policy decisions, and trying to prevent catastrophic outcomes.

[h2] Development Story
[p] p(Doom)1 began as an exploration of how games can communicate complex ideas about AI Safety and existential risks. The project evolved from simple prototypes into a comprehensive strategy game that balances entertainment with education.

[h4] Core Systems
