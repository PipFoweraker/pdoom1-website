[h1] Cat Custodians
[p] Meet the feline guardians of AI Safety. These cats have been immortalized in p(Doom)1 as reward NPCs for community contributors.
[h2] About the Cat Custodians
[p] In p(Doom)1, community contributors can earn special rewards including having a cat NPC named after them (or their cat!). These loyal felines serve as office companions, providing morale boosts and occasional insights into AI Safety research.
[p] Want to become a custodian? Check out the contributors page to learn how to earn rewards.
